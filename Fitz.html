About The Fitzwilliam Museum

History

Founded in 1816, The Fitzwilliam Museum is the art and antiquities museum of Cambridge located on Trumpington Street.

When to visit

Closed on Mondays, but open every other day of the week, The Fitz is the perfect location to grab some culture at the heart of Cambridge during your visit. For opening times see here.

What's On?

Additional to the permanent collections, The Fitzwilliam Museum hosts exhibitions and events throughout the year. To see the exhibits held during the course of your stay, take a look at their website.
