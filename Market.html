About the Market Square

History

Market Square has worked as a marketplace since Saxon times, and still continues to be opened daily; it is a popular attraction for locals, students and tourists alike.

What is there?

The market has stalls offering a variety of products; from fresh fruit, vegetables and flowers, books, clothing, jewellery and homewares, you are sure to find something to your taste here.

How to get there

Extremely close to King's Parade, the market is right at the heart of the city. You will be able to spot it from afar due to the technicolor drapery.

Tips

If you're after fresh produce, the market sells fruit and vegetables cheaper than the surrounding supermarkets. If you're after a sweet treat, the belgian waffles sold in the market are said to be 'divine'.
